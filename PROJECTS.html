    IOT BASED INDUSTRIAL PROTECTION SYSTEM

    My second poroject was IOT based industrial protection system. I had the opportunity to present this project in the ECUBE(project presentation) which was conducted in our college.

    COMPONENTS USED

  • ESP32, Temperature sensor
  • PIR motion sensor
  • GAS sensor,Buzzer
  • Relay

    • SOFTWARE USED

  • ARDUINO IDE
  • BLYNK

    • The IoT-based Industrial Safety Monitoring System is a cutting-edge solution that employs sensors and a network of connected devices to monitor and control various industrial processes. This system ensures the safety of workers and the environment by detecting potential hazards and taking preventive measures. The core of the system is an ESP32 DEVKIT module, chosen for its robust WIFI and Bluetooth connectivity capabilities. This project demonstrates the effective use of 3 sensors - DHT11 temperature sensor, MQ135 gas sensor, and PIR motion sensor - to measure critical parameters. Additionally, it utilizes 3 buzzers and a relay module for timely notifications. The system triggers the buzzers and alerts the users when threshold limits are exceeded for temperature or gas levels. It also detects any unauthorized personnel by activating the buzzer when movement is detected near the PIR sensor. Moreover, real-time data can be monitored remotely using a Blynk application on both PC and mobile devices, providing a user-friendly interface for seamless monitoring. This project serves as a stepping stone, inspiring further exploration and development in the exciting field of electronics and IoT-based applications.

    EARLY DETECTION OF PROSTATE CANCER

    It is a sensor-based bio-medical detection device that detects tumours and cancer in the prostate gland by means of high-intensity focused ultrasound and low-intensity pulsed ultrasound transmission and reception.

    It is an innovative devices which utilizes concepts of sensors, bioelectronics and medical electronics as per requirement to achieve desired outcome. It is practically a detection tool that is equipped with peizoelectric protrusion that plays a major role in emitting ultrasonic rays in target region. By receiving the ray the place of obstruction is noted in the display. Cancerous and tumorous protruision can be observed and marked by positioning the rays that are not received back due to these obstruction.
